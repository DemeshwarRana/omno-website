No shortcuts

We're not going to promise you'll be fluent in 30 days.

Most language apps sell a fantasy — gamified streaks, cute mascots, a dopamine loop that feels like progress but doesn't build a language. We didn't build that.

OmNo is built around what actually works: structured vocabulary and grammar organized by real-life topics, genuine reading practice, speaking exercises that make you say the words out loud, and exit exams you actually have to pass to move up a level — not just "complete" a lesson.

If a feature doesn't teach you German, it's not in the app. No fake promises. No shortcuts.

01

One path, every level

A single swipeable dome takes you through the whole journey — Alphabet, Vocabulary, Grammar, and real-life Scenarios, organized A1 through C2. You always know exactly where you are and what's next.

02

A dictionary that teaches, not just lists

1,183 words and counting, each with a real example sentence — not a flat translation. Filter by CEFR level, and every word links back to how it's actually used.

03

Real reading, with a safety net

Full passages in German, tap any underlined word for its meaning, or flip to the full English translation when you're stuck. Built for reading real German, not simplified textbook filler.

04

Conversations you'll actually have

Practice real scenarios — ordering at a shop, answering the phone, handling bureaucracy — with guided dialogue that shows you exactly how a native speaker would say it.

05

Exams that mean something

Comprehension checks and full exit exams modeled on the real CEFR format. Passing one is how you actually move to the next level — not a formality, a real bar.

🚧 Coming soon 06

An AI tutor, when you're stuck

Ask questions, get explanations, and talk through tricky grammar with an AI conversation partner — built, tested, and on the way. We're holding it back until it's genuinely ready, not shipping it half-finished just to check a box.
